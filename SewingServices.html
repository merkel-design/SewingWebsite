    • Quilting Services

    Our Long arm Team treats every quilt as our own. We use the Baby Lock Regalia with ProStitcher to perform edge-to-edge long arm machine quilting. Quilting price is based on $.02 per square inch.

    Prepping Your Quilt Top

    When you drop off your quilt, you have options to customize by selecting the quilting pattern, thread color, and batting. Or, if you prefer, you can request "Quilters Choice" to allow our Team to decide. Urban Spools carries two types of premium quality batting - 100% wool or 100% cotton. The brand we carry has been thoroughly tested for drape, softness, and quality. We use wool and 100% cotton blends. In general, it takes approximately 4 - 5 weeks for a quilt to be completed. Upon drop off, our Team will be able to give a more accurate turnaround time based on our current queue. If you have an urgent need, we do have a rush service for an additional fee.

    Our Machine Quilting Process

    Before you send in your quilt top to be quilted, you’ll select a quilting pattern and thread color, as well as a batting preference. You may also choose to send in your own batting if you wish. Once you complete your order, you’ll receive the following email updates from us: Order confirmation. Then, you send us your quilt top! We've received your quilt top and it's safe and sound. Your quilt top is being quilted on one of our state-of-the-art longarm quilting machines. We've finished your quilt and it is being shipped back to you. It's that simple!

    To ensure a smooth quilting process, we recommend the following steps to prepare your quilt:

    • • The max quilt top width we support is 120".
    • • Trim and remove all basting seams, points, and excess threads. ".
    • • Press all seams flat.
    • • Make sure all seams are securely sewn.
    • • Quilt top, batting and backing should be separated - not basted together.
    • • If there is a seam in the backing, it is best if it runs horizontally.
    • • Square up the quilt top by checking top, bottom, and sides are parallel to each other and corners are 90° angles.
    • • Backing fabric must be a minimum of 8” wider and longer than your quilt top to ensure 4" extra on every side. This allows sufficient room to attach the backing to the quilt frame.
    • • To determine the square inches of your quilt top, multiply the length x width. There is a minimum charge of $50.00.
    • • Any quilt not picked up within 60 days of completion will become the property of Urban Spools Sewing Lounge.
